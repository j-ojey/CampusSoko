CampusSoko

A student-to-student marketplace for buying and selling goods and services across campus.

To empower students across Kimathi to buy, sell, and hustle smarter by creating a trusted campus-to-campus digital marketplace.

About Us

We started this platform after seeing how hard it was for students to safely buy and sell things on campus. Too many lost items, sketchy deals, and unfair prices. As students ourselves, we built a marketplace just for our community — a safe, verified space where students can connect, trade, and save without the hassle.
